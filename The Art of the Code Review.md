Most code reviews check syntax. Good code reviews transfer architecture. This video is about the difference — what makes a review a learning moment vs. a gatekeeping moment, and how to give feedback that actually sticks.

## What good looks like

- Leading with questions, not corrections
- When to block, when to approve-with-nit, when to just merge
- The "author's intent" test before leaving a comment
- Async vs sync review: which to use and when
- How to review 2000-line PRs without losing your mind (don't: break them up first)

Includes a before/after of an actual review exchange showing how tone changes reception without changing the underlying point.
